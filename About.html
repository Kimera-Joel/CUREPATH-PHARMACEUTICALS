About CurePath Pharmaceutical

Our history, mission and commitment to affordable healthcare

Background Information

CurePath pharmaceutical began in 2003 by Dr. Joe Jonas and Dr. Edward Hart who had come as volunteers of the Red Cross in 2000 The shortage and high prices of medicine inspired them to come up with an idea to adress those problems and so they created CurePath Medical Center in Wantoni,Mukono and would import drugs from outside and provide it to people at affordable prices On 16th May 2003 Dr.Joe Jonas and Dr.Edward Hart commissioned the first factory in Jinja and from then on CurePath pharmaceutical has never looked back But as time went on we evolved from just producing high quality affordable medication to providing different medical services at affordable prices for everyone We also expanded a to other areas of the country where we have different branches such as Gulu, Soroti, Mpigi, Masaka, Mbale, Mbarara e.t.c.

Our Mission

To make quality healthcare accessible to everyone by delivering trusted medicines and promoting wellness across all communities.

Our Vision

To build a healthier world where every person has access to reliable and affordable medicines.

Our Goals

  • To improve access to medicine
  • To promote health awareness
  • To support local healthcare systems
  • To maintain high product quality
  • To encourage innovation for local needs
  • To sustain environmental responsibility
  • To empower through employment
